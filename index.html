Welcome

The current site keeps track of my latest publications and additionally, serves as an outlet to disseminate some of “failed projects”. I am a big believer in transparency and believe that sharing data and statistical code is an absolute necessity. The data and code for my studies is available on open science and the current site contains links to each. Furthermore, the filedrawer tab gives at least some indication to some of the projects that I have ran in the past but which I am no longer pursuing. Whenever I find the time, I try and write a small blog post about this work.
