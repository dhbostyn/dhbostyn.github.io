PhD on ice
On Friday January 18, we will go ice-skating in the Kristallijn (Warmoezeniersweg 20, 9000 Gent). Musical beats will be provided by a live DJ, as well as a jenever bar to keep you warm. Do not miss this unique opportunity to get in touch with your colleagues and have some fun! We will gather at the entrance at 19h55.

Registration for this event is required. Subscription is completed by depositing 5.5 euro to BE52 3631 1096 4909 before January 11. Please add your email address as additional info during payment. When the payment is received, we will send you a confirmation email.

We really hope to see you all in ‘De Kristallijn’!
